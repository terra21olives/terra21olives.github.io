About Us

The ‘Olive Journey’

Through the ‘Olive Journey’ we share with the fans of Greek and World Gastronomy our passion for the Olive, one of the most important Greek products that is completely intertwined with the culture and civilization of Greek society.
In 'The Journey of the Olive' we share with the world that rare information that creates the important differences, we talk about the land, the producer, the olive tree and the place.
Through this different ‘Olive Journey’ we present the olive in recipes inspired not only by the Gastronomy of Greece but also by historical views of other countries and continents.
Let us all enjoy together a different journey, a journey of culture and taste, the ‘Olive Journey’.
