Tastes

Through the ‘Olive Journey’ we share with the fans of Greek and World Gastronomy our passion for the Olive, one of the most important Greek products that is completely intertwined with the culture and civilization of Greek society. In 'The Journey of the Olive' we share with the world that rare information that creates the important differences, we talk about the land, the producer, the olive tree and the place. Through this different ‘Olive Journey’ we present the olive in recipes inspired not only by the Gastronomy of Greece but also by historical views of other countries and continents. Let us all enjoy together a different journey, a journey of culture and taste, the ‘Olive Journey’.

Traditional Recipe

A recipe from Astakos, the place where we grew up where the preparation of olive has a single purpose: to keep all its natural characteristics and to highlight its special taste profile.

A culinary heritage given to the World by Kalamata. We keep our olives in perfumed vinegar for long time, the result is a firm texture and delicate but long natural aftertaste.

A recipe that brings together two gastronomic schools. The Minor Asia one defined by the aromas of crushed chillies and cumin and the Mediterranean culinary school defined by oregano and garlic.
